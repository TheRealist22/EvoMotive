The 4C is one of the newer models offered from the legendary manufacturer and has renvisioned the sports car with the full carbon monocoque chassis and breathtaking design. Seen in past with racing pedigree starting in Formula 1 and moving towards more road oriented cars with timeless design's with the Berera, 8C and the Disco Volante. In modern times thier two flagship models being the Quadrifoglio GTA and Stelvio.

SPECIFICATIONS

Engine 1.75 Litre Turbocharged Inline-4
Transmission 6-Speed Dual Dry Clutch
Horsepower 237 HP
Torque 258 lbs-ft
Top Speed 160 Mph
